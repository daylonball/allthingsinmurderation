All Things in Murderation


You have been stranded in snow storm...you come across an elegant ski lodge/chalet. Upon entering you come accross a group of people having a party.

Description

All Things in Murderation is a detailed Murder mystery game where your choices affect the story and give the story the ability to "branch" off to get different outcomes. Choose wisely, your choices may get someone killed!

Details

  • Murder Mystery
  • Branching Narrative
  • Story Based
  • Item Interaction
  • 2D Topdown
  • Multiple Endings

The Characters




  • Player (You)

    The Detective

    As a bright young detective, in the midst of a snow storm you somehow stumble upon a chalet. You embark on an adventure of mystery, lies, and of course murder...

  • Maggie Hatrick

    The Magician

    A dazzling young magician who is more than meets the eye...

  • Charlotte Barr

    The Chef

    The Chalet's resident chef. Mostly keeps to herself and is generally quiet. Is currently trying a new recipe for chocolate...

  • Reid Herring

    The Entrepreneur

    Owner of the mighty succesful Herrington Chocolate Company, Reid can be quite abnoxious at times.

  • Willem Van Der Berg

    The Billionare

    An excentric old billionare who happens to own the chalet!

  • Ruby Valentina

    The Bounty Hunter

    A bounty hunter and debt collector...you do not want to mess with her...

  • James Sterling

    The Professor

    A professor and scientist who currently has tenure at Yale.

  • Margaret Van Der Berg

    The Daughter

    The esteemed daughter of Willem Van Der Berg... She also happens to be married to Brennan O'Shea...

  • Buck Denim

    The Millionare

    Buck Denim is not the brightest apple in the bunch...but he is rich.

  • Brennan O'Shea

    The Pub Owner

    Of Irish decent, the husband of Margaret Van Der Berg... he also happens owns a pub...

  • Jeffrick Rey

    The Odd One

    A very mysterious looking man...nothing is really known of this person...
